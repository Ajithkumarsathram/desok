Angular is a structural framework for dynamic web apps and it lets you use HTML as your template language and extend HTML’s syntax to represent your application’s components clearly and briefly. It is fully extensible and operates well with other libraries. All the features can be modified or replaced to befit your unique development workflow and essential features.
